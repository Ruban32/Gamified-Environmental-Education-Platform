EcoQuest

Gamified Environmental Education Platform for Schools and Colleges

About the Project

EcoQuest is an educational platform that helps students learn about environmental protection through interactive activities, quizzes, and eco-friendly challenges.

Objectives

  • Create environmental awareness.
  • Encourage sustainable habits.
  • Promote eco-friendly activities.
  • Make learning fun and engaging.

Features

  • Environmental Learning Modules
  • Interactive Quizzes
  • Eco Challenges
  • Points and Rewards System
  • Student Progress Tracking

Benefits

Students can improve their environmental knowledge and develop responsible habits that contribute to a greener future.
